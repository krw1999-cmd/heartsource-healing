Upkeep

Sustain your healing journey with ongoing support and community connection.

Nurture Your Growth

Upkeep is about consistency and community—the essential ingredients for lasting transformation. After beginning your healing journey, maintaining progress requires continued practice, support, and connection with others on similar paths.

Our upkeep programs offer structured, ongoing engagement through weekly classes and multi-month coaching experiences. Here, you'll find the accountability, encouragement, and gentle guidance needed to integrate new habits and sustain positive change over time.

Choose Your Path

Weekly Pain-Free Yoga Classes

Join our welcoming community for gentle, adaptive yoga classes that honor your body's needs. Designed to relieve pain and build strength gradually, these weekly sessions provide consistent support for your physical wellness journey in a warm, judgment-free environment.
